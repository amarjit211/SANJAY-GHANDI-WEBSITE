SGNP - Sanjay Gandhi National Park

Do's and Dont's

Do's

  • Keep Silence in the Park
  • Speed Limit 20km/Hour of Fine up to Rs. 25000/-
  • Park your Vehicles at Parking Lots.
  • Leave the Park before 18.30 Hrs.
  • Use Dust Bin s, No Plastic at all.
  • Abide by rules & Regulations of national park.
  • Please validate the pass / ticket at the Gate.
  • For Commercial photography / video shooting contact ticket counter.
  • Park Timings: 7:30am to 6:30pm

Dont's

  • Don’t tease and Feed wild animals.
  • Don’t Park your Vehicles along roadside –Fine up to 2000/-
  • Don’t use /throw plastic bags in the park.
  • Lighting of fire Prohibited.
  • No alcohol / smoking.
  • Pets totally prohibited.
